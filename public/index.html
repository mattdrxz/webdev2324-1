Sweet Ice Tea Recipe

Sweet Ice Tea

Ingredient

  • Tea
    a Pouch
  • Sugar
    3 Tbsp
  • Hot Water
    a Glass
  • Ice
    6-10 Cubes

How to Make

  1. Brew the tea with hot water
  2. Put 3 Tbsp sugar to the tea water
  3. Mix it until the sugar dissolved with the tea
  4. Put some cubes of ice
  5. Voila! You're done :)
